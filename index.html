Achieve your creative and career potential.

At the world's preeminent college for the study of music, students can experience all aspects of the music industry through an interdisciplinary curriculum. Are you ready to march to the beat of your own drum? At Groove Academy, we offer a unique and exciting opportunity to explore the world of percussion and rhythm. Our drum school is more than just a place to learn; it's a community where students of all ages and skill levels come together to discover the magic of music through the power of drumming.
